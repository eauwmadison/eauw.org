Aaron Yarmel (PhD, MSc, MA) is Associate Director of the Center for Ethics and Human Values at Ohio State, where he oversees program operations and leads initiatives on dialogue facilitation, civil discourse, and the ETHOS Fellows program. His academic research spans two-level utilitarianism, social change, and the philosophy of education. He earned his PhD in Philosophy from UW–Madison, founded Madison Public Philosophy, and has been an active EA contributor since 2015. He also maintains a private consulting practice in philosophical counseling and facilitation.
