Aaron Yarmel (PhD, MSc, MA) is the Associate Director of the Center for Ethics and Human Values at The Ohio State University, where he oversees program operations and leads initiatives on dialogue facilitation, civil discourse, and the ETHOS Fellows program. His academic research spans two-level utilitarianism, social change, and the philosophy of education. He earned his PhD in Philosophy from UW–Madison and founded Madison Public Philosophy, bringing philosophical inquiry to children and the broader public. An active EA community member since 2015, Aaron has contributed through research, guest speaking, and organizing EAGx conferences. He also maintains a private consulting practice offering philosophical counseling and dialogue facilitation training.
