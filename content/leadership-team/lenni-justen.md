Lenni co-founded EA UW–Madison with his brother in 2020, helping build a community of people in Madison inspired to do good in the world. He graduated from UW–Madison in 2022 with a B.S. in Physics and Political Science and is now a PhD Candidate at MIT and the Broad Institute, advised by Pardis Sabeti and Kevin Esvelt. His research focuses on detecting emerging pathogens through wastewater metagenomics and suppressing transmission in built environments. He also serves as a Research Manager at ERA Cambridge on AI and biology, and has previously worked with the Council on Strategic Risks on bioweapons policy and with the U.S. Department of State on the bioeconomy.
