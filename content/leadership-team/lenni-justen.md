Lenni co-founded EA UW–Madison in 2020 and graduated in 2022 with a B.S. in Physics and Political Science. He is now a PhD Candidate at MIT and the Broad Institute, advised by Pardis Sabeti and Kevin Esvelt, and serves as a Research Manager at ERA Cambridge on AI and biology. His research focuses on detecting emerging pathogens through wastewater metagenomics and suppressing transmission in built environments.
