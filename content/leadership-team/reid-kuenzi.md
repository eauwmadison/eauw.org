Reid joined EA UW leadership in 2022 after completing the Intro Fellowship and served as President from 2024 to 2025. He graduated in 2024 with a degree in Electrical Engineering (Machine Learning & Data Science) and a certificate in German. His interest in AI safety led him to serve as Head of Policy for the Wisconsin AI Safety Initiative and as a Research Assistant for the Center for AI Policy, drafting briefs for congressional staffers on FDA-style AI regulations. He now works as a Public Service Engineer for the Public Service Commission of Wisconsin and remains passionate about helping others maximize their positive impact—when he's not sailing, reading, or getting into deep philosophical discussions with friends.
