Reid first joined EA UW leadership in 2022 after completing the Intro to Effective Altruism Fellowship, eventually serving as President from 2024 to 2025. Reid graduated in 2024 with a degree in Electrical Engineering (focusing on Machine Learning and Data Science) and a certificate in German. His deep interest in AI safety led him to serve as the Head of Policy for the Wisconsin AI Safety Initiative and work as a Research Assistant for the Center for AI Policy, where he researched FDA-style regulations for AI and drafted briefs for congressional staffers. Currently, Reid works as a Public Service Engineer for the Public Service Commission of Wisconsin. He remains passionate about helping others use their careers for maximal positive impact and still finds time for sailing, reading, and deep philosophical discussions with friends.
