Michel co-founded EA UW in Fall 2020 after hearing a podcast episode—as one does. Alongside his twin, Lenni, and a dedicated team of early organizers, he helped shape the group into a community of standout people committed to leaving the world better than they found it. Since graduating from UW-Madison in 2022 with degrees in Neurobiology and Psychology, Michel has moved to San Francisco to work at the intersection of AI safety and infrastructure. After a stint running high-level events for the Centre for Effective Altruism and researching international benefit-sharing at Pivotal Research, he has landed at the Tarbell Center for AI Journalism. As of early 2026, he serves as their AI Grants Lead, supporting journalism that helps society navigate the emergence of advanced AI.
