Akhil is a PhD student in Cybersecurity at University College London (UCL), where he works within the Center for Doctoral Training to mitigate technology-facilitated abuse in Smart Home and IoT ecosystems. He holds a Master’s in Computer Science from UW–Madison and a Bachelor’s from the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati. He has been actively involved with the effective altruism community for several years now through the facilitation of virtual programs. He is especially interested in reducing existential risks from Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and Engineered Pandemics.
