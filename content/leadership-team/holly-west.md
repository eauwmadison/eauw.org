Holly graduated from UW–Madison with degrees in Economics and International Studies and a certificate in Art History. Her interest in global welfare led her to a research internship with the World Justice Project in Washington, D.C., where she focused on the rule of law and international development. She remains especially passionate about reducing extreme global poverty and the fight against preventable diseases. Currently, Holly is working as a Business Development Assistant at Kirkland & Ellis. Outside EA, she enjoys yoga, going for walks and hikes, trying new recipes, and hanging out with friends.
